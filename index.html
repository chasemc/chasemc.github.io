Backgound

I received my Bachelors in Biochemistry from Berry Collge where I was a member of the honors program and a Bonner scholar. After graduating I worked at Deerland Enzymes as an R&D scientist. I recently finished my PhD in Pharmacognosy at the Universtiy of Illinois at Chicago where I studied under an F31 fellowship in the lab of Brian Murphy. I am currently a a Postdoctoral Research Associate in Jason Kwan's lab at the University of Wisconsin-Madison where I work on bioinformatics, metagenomics, etc.

Also, impatiently waiting for another scientific expedition or even just a weekend whitewater kayaking trip.
